After @andrew.cmu.edu got cross realm, the admin needs to give permission to the folders they need to edit.

Add user to adegani:modsnake pts group (or another group):

pts adduser @andrew.cmu.edu adegani:modsnake

this should automatically add premissions to these two folders.

Check membership:
pts membership adegani:modsnake

other groups:
adegani:16311admin
adegani:admin

To add individual user to all subdirs:
this gives full permissions (rlidwka) to projects/modsnake and to www/modsnake and all of its subdirectories:
see:http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~help/afs/afs_acls.html for more info.

find /afs/cs.cmu.edu/project/biorobotics/www/projects/modsnake -type d -print | xargs fs sa -acl @andrew.cmu.edu all -dir

or

find /afs/cs.cmu.edu/project/biorobotics/www/projects/modsnake -type d -exec fs sa -acl @andrew.cmu.edu all -dir '{}' ';'

and also do:

find /afs/cs.cmu.edu/project/biorobotics/www/modsnake -type d -exec fs sa -acl @andrew.cmu.edu all -dir '{}' ';'
